The Hidden Cost of Airbnb

If you've ever booked on Airbnb, you've seen the sticker shock at checkout. That $80/night listing suddenly becomes $115/night after fees. Here's why:

  • Airbnb guest service fee: 14-16% of the subtotal
  • Cleaning fee: Set by the host, often $50-100
  • Airbnb host fee: 3% taken from the host (who raises prices to compensate)

On a 3-night stay at $80/night, Airbnb adds roughly $35-50 in service fees alone. That's money that goes to a Silicon Valley corporation — not to improving your stay.

When you book the exact same property direct, those fees disappear. The host can offer a lower price because they're not paying Airbnb's commission, and you don't pay the service fee. Everyone wins except Airbnb.
