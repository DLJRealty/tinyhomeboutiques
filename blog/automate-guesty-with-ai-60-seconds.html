How to Automate Your Guesty Property Management with AI in 60 Seconds

Stop toggling between 15 tabs. Let AI handle your Guesty operations while you focus on growing your portfolio.

The Problem Every Guesty Host Knows

You bought Guesty to streamline your property management. And it does — Guesty is powerful. But powerful also means complex. You still spend hours every week switching between reservations, calendars, messaging, and financials. Manually responding to guest messages at 2 AM. Pulling financial reports across multiple properties.

What if you could do all of that with a single command?

Introducing Guesty Copilot — The First AI-Powered Guesty Integration

Guesty Copilot is an open-source MCP server that connects your Guesty account to AI assistants like Claude, Cursor, and Windsurf. It gives your AI 39 tools to manage every aspect of your property business. One command to install. Zero configuration headaches. Free forever.

Setup: Literally 60 Seconds

Step 1: Open your terminal and run:

npx guesty-mcp-server

Step 2: Add your Guesty API credentials (Client ID and Secret from your Guesty dashboard under Marketplace > API).

Step 3: Connect to your AI assistant by adding the config to your Claude Code settings. That's it.
