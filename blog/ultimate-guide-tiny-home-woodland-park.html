The Ultimate Guide to Tiny Home Stays in Woodland Park, Colorado

March 30, 2026 · Boutique mountain living with direct Pikes Peak views

Forget the cookie-cutter hotel room. Forget the overcrowded resort. If you want to actually feel Colorado — the mountain air, the sunrise over Pikes Peak, the sound of nothing but wind through pine trees — a tiny home stay in Woodland Park is the move.

Tiny homes offer something hotels simply can't: privacy, character, and an experience you'll remember. You get your own hot tub, your own fire pit, your own front-row seat to one of America's most famous mountains. And you get it at a fraction of what a luxury resort charges.

What Makes Woodland Park Special

Woodland Park sits at 8,500 feet elevation in the shadow of Pikes Peak — the most visited mountain in North America. It's a small mountain town with big access:

  • 25 minutes from Colorado Springs
  • 90 minutes from Denver
  • 10 minutes from the Pikes Peak Highway
  • 20 minutes from Garden of the Gods
  • 15 minutes from Cave of the Winds
  • 20 minutes from Manitou Springs

Unlike staying in Colorado Springs or Denver, Woodland Park puts you in the mountains, not looking at them from a distance. You wake up at elevation, surrounded by Ponderosa pines, with 14,115-foot Pikes Peak filling your window.

What to Do During Your Stay

Day 1: Arrive and Unwind

  • Check in and settle into your tiny home
  • Fire up the hot tub and watch the sunset behind Pikes Peak
  • Cook dinner in your full kitchen or walk to Woodland Park's local restaurants
  • Start the fire pit and stargaze at 8,500 feet (the stars are insane up here)

Day 2: Explore

  • Morning: Drive up the Pikes Peak Highway (America's Mountain — 14,115 feet)
  • Afternoon: Explore Garden of the Gods (free, 20 minutes away)
  • Evening: Visit Manitou Springs for dinner and the quirky downtown shops

Day 3: Adventure

  • Hike: Mueller State Park, Rampart Reservoir, or Catamount Trail
  • Visit: Cave of the Winds, North Pole Santa's Workshop, or Dinosaur Resource Center
  • Relax: One more hot tub session before checkout
